About UMD

There are so many things I like about Duluth. One of my favorites things in Duluth is the scenery and hikes, I love to do outdoor activities and going on the hikes around Duluth is very enjoyable for me. Additionally, there is so much to do in town like different events, festivals, and restaurants that are enjoyable.

UMD Campus Highlights

The UMD campus has so many things to do, one of my favorite things about UMD is that everything is connected, so in the cold winter I don't have to walk outside. Additionally, the gym is very well-maintained with lots of machines and weights to workout,something I am very passionate about. Lastly, the food options around campus are one of my absolute favorite things about UMD, there are numerous delicious and diverse options to offer around campus from the dining hall to the food court!
